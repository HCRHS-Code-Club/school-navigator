How data is stored

<hallways> - a list of intraconnected hallways
<hallway id=[int] > - a hallway identified by a number containing rooms, and entrances
<entrance> - contained within a hallway
<north/south/east/west id=[int] > contained within an entrance, represents hallways connected at this entrance, cardinal direction the connected hallway goes
<rooms> - a group of rooms within a hallway
<room number=[int] > - a room within a hallway
